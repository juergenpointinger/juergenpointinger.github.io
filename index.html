About me

I was born in Linz (Upper Austria, AUT) in 1984. With loving support from my mom and stepdad, I grew up in a small town near Linz. I spent my childhood on sports pitches, where I started to love soccer and became a huge Bayern Munich (GER) fan.

After almost 25 years in Upper Austria I met the love of my life and followed her to Vienna (AUT) in 2009. Since then I live in the most beautiful and most livable city in the world. Our 2 boys make our family happiness complete. They make me proud every day!

At Nagarro I can live out my entrepreneurial spirit and accompany my clients on their journey of change so they can get closer to their business goals. Some claim that I am a good leader and mentor. I want to thank them a lot and I try to return this trust every day.
